Hi, I’m Josephine

Game designer and media psychologist

I recently graduated MSc Medialogy with specialization in Games from Aalborg University, Copenhagen. During my semester at University of Salford, I also obtained PgCert Media Psychology.

Games have always been very natural to me, and it has always been a part of my everyday life. Through my studies, I have focused on how people interact with games and how to design, test and implement new elements or designs.

During my semester abroad, I got to focus on the psychological aspects of media and how these can be applied to games. A certain strength of mine is, that I do not quit until the product works.

I am looking for new opportunities within the game industry.

Competences

Game design

My specialty is using psychology to immmerse the player. I am interested in serious games, identification and continuation desire. I am intrigued by new interfaces and their challenges, e.g. Virtual Reality.

Technical skills

I have used Unity3D and Unreal Engine, but I have also developed interactive narratives through Advanced Story Builder. I have experience with Java, C#, HTML, Xcode, Swift and Photoshop at user level.

Media Psychology

I attended a semester at University of Salford, where I studied Media Psychology. Here I got to specialize myself in how psychology can be applied to games and media to enhance the general experience.

Data analysis

I am used to analysing data obtained from both qualitative and quantitative tests. I have also worked with target group analysis and site statistics - both which I am used to presenting and communicating to the rest of the office.
